Historical Places

City Hall

The City Hall currently holds Parliament sittings and located centrally in Town. City Hall is a sight to see for its beautiful architecture. City Hall has tight security and its perimeters are higly guarded.

Castle Of Good Hope

The Castle was, however, not the first fort to be built at the Cape. A quadrangular (four-pointed) fort was built after the arrival of Jan van Riebeeck in 1652 where the Grand Parade and the main Post Office are situated today.

Iziko Museum

The South African Museum, founded in 1825 and situated in the historic Company’s Gardens, has seen millions of visitors, attracted by the vast historical collection the museum has to offer, ranging from fossils to ancient insects and historical tools.
